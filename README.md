# 🚖 Uber Ride Booking Analytics | Power BI Business Intelligence Project

> An end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI, Excel, Power Query, and DAX** to analyze Uber ride-booking performance, operational efficiency, customer demand, and revenue trends.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Uber ride-booking data and transforms raw transactional records into interactive business insights through an executive Power BI dashboard.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor booking performance, revenue generation, fleet utilization, booking fulfillment, and customer demand across multiple vehicle categories. It provides decision-makers with real-time KPIs, trend analysis, and actionable recommendations for improving operational efficiency.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Ride-sharing platforms generate massive amounts of operational data every day. Without an effective analytics solution, it becomes difficult to:

* Monitor revenue performance
* Track booking fulfillment rates
* Identify high-performing vehicle categories
* Understand customer demand patterns
* Optimize fleet allocation
* Detect operational bottlenecks
* Improve business profitability

This dashboard converts raw booking data into meaningful business intelligence for strategic decision-making.

# 📌 Key Business Insights

## 🚕 Auto Generates the Highest Revenue

* Revenue: **₹12.88M**
* Completed Bookings: **23K**

Auto is the strongest performing category due to affordability, accessibility, and high booking frequency.

---

## 🏍 Bike Shows Strong Growth Potential

* Revenue: **₹11.46M**
* Completed Bookings: **21K**

Bike rides demonstrate excellent operational efficiency and high adoption in traffic-congested urban areas.

---

## 🚗 Go Mini Maintains Stable Demand

* Revenue: **₹10.34M**
* Completed Bookings: **19K**

Go Mini offers the right balance between affordability and customer comfort.

---

## 💎 Premier Sedan Generates Higher Revenue Per Ride

* Revenue: **₹6.28M**
* Completed Bookings: **11K**

Although ride volume is lower, Premier Sedan delivers significantly higher revenue per booking, making it an attractive premium segment.

---

## ⚠️ Lost Bookings Represent Revenue Leakage

* Lost Bookings: **57K**
* Lost Booking Rate: **38%**

Reducing lost bookings can significantly increase annual revenue without increasing customer acquisition costs.

---

## 📍 High Demand Locations

Top-performing pickup locations include:

* Khandsa
* Badarpur
* Jahangirpuri
* Jasola
* Vaishali

These locations should receive priority driver allocation and operational planning.
